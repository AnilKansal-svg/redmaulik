Wood Protection

MAULIK is a worldwide leading manufacturer of biocidal active ingredients and formulations and offers a wide range of products for many different applications.

Trusted and innovative technologies protect wood from mould, moisture, insects, and fungi while enhancing its natural appearance. These solutions penetrate deep into the wood, increasing durability and preserving its grain and color over time. Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, they help extend the lifespan of wood products, reduce maintenance needs, and support sustainable construction by minimizing material waste.

Markets

Fungicides

Our fungicides offer broad-spectrum efficacy against wood-destroying and wood-staining fungi, as well as mildew. Engineered to prevent biodegradation, they help maintain the structural integrity and aesthetic quality of wood, minimizing both economic loss and material deterioration.

Insecticides

Our insecticides provide targeted protection against a range of wood-destroying insects, including termites. Formulated to prevent structural damage caused by wood-chewing pests, they help preserve the quality and value of wood materials by minimizing degradation and associated economic losses.

Industrial Wood Protection

For wood protection formulations, high-performance fungicidal and insecticidal/termiticidal active agents are available for use in water and various solvents. These can be applied using a range of industrial methods, including brushing, dipping, flow coating, or through vacuum and pressure impregnation in stationary woodworking systems.

Wood Composites
