Markets

Beamhouse

Beamhouse solutions optimize every stage from soaking to deliming, enhancing leather quality while reducing water use and environmental impact. Designed for efficiency and compliance, our processes support sustainable, high-performance leather production.

Degreasing and Scouring

Maulik provides advanced degreasing and scouring solutions for all hides, including high-fat types, ensuring efficient fat removal, clean pelts, and low effluent impact—optimized for broad pH and low-temperature processing.

Tanning

Maulik delivers advanced tanning systems for chrome-free, metal-free, and aldehyde-free leather, ensuring high shrinkage temperature, tight grain, and clean shaving. Our high-exhaustion technologies reduce COD load and enhance sustainability without compromising leather performance.

Fatliquoring

Maulik delivers advanced fatliquoring solutions for soft, strong, and durable leather. Designed for low emissions and high sustainability, our formulations enhance tear strength, water repellence, and lightweight comfort across all leather types.

Retanning

Maulik retanning solutions enhance leather fullness, tightness, and softness using advanced, formaldehyde-free polymers. Designed for eco-friendly performance, they ensure selective filling and meet the highest sustainability standards.
