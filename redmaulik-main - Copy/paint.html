Paints & Coatings

MAULIK is a worldwide leading manufacturer of biocidal active ingredients and formulations and offers a wide range of products for many different applications.

We provide a full suite of advanced solutions including marine antifouling, dry-film and wet-state protection, plant hygiene, and performance additives. These technologies are designed to enhance product durability, prevent microbial contamination, and support clean manufacturing—all while meeting modern standards for sustainability, efficiency, and regulatory compliance.

Markets

Marine Antifouling

Arxada’s Omadine® and UmiGard® technologies provide long-lasting, regulatory-compliant protection against marine biofoulers. Omadine® targets slime and algae, while UmiGard® enhances the sustained release of actives, ensuring durable performance in underwater coatings.

Read More

Dry-Film Protection

Arxada’s Omadine® and Densil® dry-film preservatives offer long-lasting, broad-spectrum protection against fungi, yeast, and algae. Available in liquid and powder forms, they ensure color stability, zero VOCs, and easy formulation due to high solubility.

Read More

Wet State Protection

Microbial control in metalworking fluids is crucial to prevent issues like corrosion, foul odors, and clogged filters—especially with the industry shift to water-based fluids. Effective biocontrol solutions, combined with defoamers and detection kits, help maintain fluid integrity and ensure smooth operations.

Read More

Plant Hygiene

Antimicrobial solutions help enhance durability and hygiene across diverse applications, from bedding and carpets to healthcare and technical textiles.

Read More

Performance additives

We offer advanced antimicrobial solutions for the leather industry, providing broad-spectrum protection and options free from TCTB, formaldehyde, and carbendazim. From beamhouse to post-treatment, our products ensure safety and durability at every stage of production.
