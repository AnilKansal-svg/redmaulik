Textile

MAULIK is a worldwide leading manufacturer of biocidal active ingredients and formulations and offers a wide range of products for many different applications.

We understand that every consumer is unique, which is why our solutions are designed to meet a wide range of personal care needs. Our focus on performance, safety, and environmental responsibility ensures the development of high-quality cosmetic products that align with evolving market expectations.
